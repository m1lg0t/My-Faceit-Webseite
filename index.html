FPL

To join the FACEIT Pro League (FPL), players typically need to progress through several stages, starting with Master League on Faceit. Reaching Level 10 on FACEIT and purchasing Premium grants access to Master League, where players earn or lose Elo points based on performance. The top players in Master League then qualify for FPLC (FPL Challenger), and the best performers in FPLC can earn a spot in FPL.
